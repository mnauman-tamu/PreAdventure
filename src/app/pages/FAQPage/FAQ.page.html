Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What does this application do?

This web application aids you in planning a trip or vacation. We give you the top points of interest, best flights to take, and the crime rate of the place that you will be visited. All of this information is available in one page. There is no need to search through several websites and find the information that you need. Our goal is to provide one place where you can save time doing research about a city.

How can I use this application?

Head to the home page and enter in a US city as your origin location and a US city as your destination. You will be directed to a summary page with all the details that we provide about your destination. You can click on the dropdown sections and explore the information has to offer you. If you want more details on a specific section, there are more details pages that you can look at to get more information.

What places can I search for?

Currently this application is only able to search for places in the United States. You can search for any place in the US, but some smaller cities may not work as well since there is not a lot of data for those places. This website works best for travel destinations that have some points of interest.
How can I book a hotel?

You can book a hotel by going to the Hotel more details page and clicking on the name of the hotel which will redirect you to the trip advisor hotel booking site. There you will be able to put in the dates that you want to book the hotel for and book it from that site.

Who can I contact if I have more questions?

If you have more questions, head to the Contact Us page and enter your question in the form that we have. We would love to answer any further questions that you have.
